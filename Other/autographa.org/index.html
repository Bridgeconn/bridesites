The Autographa Project

About


The Autographa Project is an initiative of Friends of Agape. FOA are business professionals, church leaders, individuals and families who team-up with Christians serving in challenging places of the world. Our focus is the South Asia region, partnering primarily with ministries in India, Nepal and Bhutan where 35% of the of the world’s unreached people groups live.

Vision


  • ■ To provide simple, incredibly easy-to-use, open source, scripture translation and publishing tools to Mother tongue translators in the context of Church Centric Bible Translation movements worldwide.
  • ■ To equip the bible translation community with a free, state of the art, minimalist, yet effective alternative to existing USFM software designed specially with the technologically challenged Mother tongue translator (MTT) in mind.
  • ■ Reduce to minimum the time for onboarding new translators
  • ■ Keep application ultra lightweight and customizable by keeping code open source.

Why another translation tool?

Free

Autographa is competely free to download and use for anyone who wants to work with scripture translation.

Easy to use

Autographa has a very minimal learning curve and easy to onboard new translators due to its elegant UX.

Multilingual

Autographa's User Interface is localized into Hindi,Arabic, Portuguese and Spanish.

Cross-Platform

Autographa is now available for Windows, Linux or MacOS desktop operating systems.

MTT Centric

Autographa is written from scratch with the user i.e. Mother tongue translator in mind.

CCBT ready

Autographa is highly suited to the CCBT translation paradigm where users may not be very tech savyy.

Success Story



What started as small summer project in 2016, as just a proof of concept of an open source alternative to proprietary translation tools, is now a well recognized tool in CCBT circles in several parts of the world. Autographa has received unprecented support and success and today we have 500 plus downloads of the recent versions and are encouraged by the response. Within our knowledge around 50 projects are being done using Autographa and related tools. Autographa is recognized by several BT agencies worldwide as an easy to use translation editor.
