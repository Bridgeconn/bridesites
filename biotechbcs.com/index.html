What is MOSS (Medical Oxygen Supply System)

Medical Oxygen Supply System (MOSS) is a standalone oxygen generator designed to replace high pressure cylinders used in hospitals and other healthcare institutions. MOSS is an innovative solution in the industry, providing uninterrupted medical grade oxygen supply with excellent reliability, ease of operation, safety and cost effectiveness with potential for further expansion. If required, MOSS can also be configured to work alongside an existing cylinder based system..

Advantages of MOSS Generators

Disadvantages Of Conventional Cylinders Advantages Of MOSS Generators
Requires frequent replacement and refilling. One time installation and uninterrupted oxygen supply from real-time oxygen generators.
High operational cost and require periodic monitoring. Low operational costs and requires minimal monitoring.
Requires large space for storage of cylinders. Compact dimensions occupy less space.
High risk of fire and explosion. Minimal risk.
Risk of injuries while handling and transportation. Requires no regular handling or transportation.
High-pressure gas storage facilities demand extra safety measure. Safer system.
Possible compromise on the purity of oxygen supply. High purity system.
Risk of cost escalation. Fixed cost.
Risk of infection due to reuse of cylinders among hospitals after refills. No risk of infection from oxygen system.
